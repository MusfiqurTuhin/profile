About me

I am Md. Musfiqur Rahman (Tuhin), a passionate and creative Graphic & Web Designer with a strong foundation in Deep Learning and Data Science, alongside substantial experience leading volunteer initiatives.

My academic work reflects my commitment to leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) for solving real-world problems. I have authored and co-authored three peer-reviewed papers published in major international conferences. I authored a peer-reviewed paper titled Plant Disease Recognition from the Perspective of Bangladesh: A Comparative Study of Deep Learning Models and Ensemble Techniques (IEEE Xplore, ECCE2025) , where the ensemble model achieved an impressive 95% accuracy for early detection; Additionally, my research paper Bengali Fake News Detection: A Multi-Layered LSTM Ensemble Approach (IEEE Xplore, QPAIN 2025) , which resulted in 82.43% accuracy in detecting misinformation; and my comprehensive study, A Comparative Analysis of Various Deep Learning Models for Traffic Signs Recognition from the Perspective of Bangladesh (Springer Nature, BIM 2023), which involved creating a novel Bangladeshi traffic sign dataset and demonstrated ViT achieved 99.91% accuracy.

With six years of experience in the design industry, I specialize in crafting compelling visuals and intuitive digital experiences. My expertise spans all areas of graphic design, brand identity development, and creating user-centric web interfaces that seamlessly blend creativity and functionality. After operating as a Level 1 Freelancer on Fiverr until 2024 with consistent 5/5 star ratings, I now focus exclusively on my role as an Independent Freelancer, delivering end-to-end branding and website design solutions for clients across multiple industries.

My volunteer journey is equally significant; I founded and preside over the Muktanchol Sahittya Chorcha Kendro and previously served as the Founder General Secretary of Ovizatri, contributing meaningfully to professional projects and social causes. Beyond design and AI research, I excel in content writing, crafting narratives that captivate audiences and drive engagement.

Let’s connect and explore opportunities to collaborate on exciting projects or initiatives. I’m always eager to take on new challenges and opportunities!
